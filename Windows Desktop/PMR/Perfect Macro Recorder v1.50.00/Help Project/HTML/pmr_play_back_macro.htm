Play back macro

 Top  Previous  Next

To play a recorded macro choose the macro name from the combo box then click Play back button or press the global hot keys combination of the macro you want to play back if you assigned one when you saved the macro or right Click Perfect Macro Recorder  System Tray Icon and go to Play back menu then choose the macro name from the sub-menu.

 

Note:

•Press Pause Button if you want to pause Play back.
•Press Alt+Insert to Resume Play back.

 

•If you wish to Stop playback you can choose any of the following shortcut keys:

 

        Press "Alt+F10"       Perfect Macro Recorder playback global hot key

        Press "Alt-Ctrl-Del"
