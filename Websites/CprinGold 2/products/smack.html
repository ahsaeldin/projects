Overview of Smack v1.06

Smack Macro Recorder records keystrokes, records mouse events, and clicks. Okay that's sound good what is the next step? You can replay these activites [keystrokes and mouse events] later. wait... You can also convert this macro to an executable file [*.exe] that can be run stand alone without Smack Macro recorder hence you can use Smack to automate any task.

How it works:

  • Open Smack and click Record New Macro button and perform the task you want to automate.
  • Hit Alt+F10 to stop recording mode, name the new macro, and save the macro.
  • Replay the macro to perform the task by pressing Playback button. Smack will accomplish the task.
