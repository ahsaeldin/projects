VB 6.0, VC++ 6.0, VB.NET, VC#.NET, VJ#.NET

Overview of Macro Recorder ActiveX control v1.50

MRAC is an ActiveX control provides an easy way to enable your application written in VB, VC++, C#,VB.NET, or other COM environment to record and playback mouse clicks, keystrokes and save them into a file in order to playback later.
