Settings

To configure Perfect Macro Recorder press the "Settings" button:

 

In General Tab, you can choose between three options for recording :

 

 

•

 

 

Record All mouse activities : records mouse movements & clicks

•

Record keyboard activities : records keyboard keystrokes

 

For General Options, you have set of standard options, starting Perfect Macro Recorder at Windows startup and auto recording a new macro upon starting Perfect Macro Recorder.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

•

Repeat times :  enables you to repeat the playing of a macro.

•

Playback speed

 

•

Notify me of playback ending

•

•

Enable smart playback : by enable this check box, Perfect Macro Recorder will search for every window you perform the recording activities for, and activate it to accurate playing of the macro as mush as possible.
