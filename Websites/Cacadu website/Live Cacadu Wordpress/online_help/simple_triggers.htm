Simple Triggers

Triggers are events that execute at a time and frequency specified by the user. To create a trigger,

 

first select the task that will be executed and click on the "Triggers" button.

 

Next, click on the "Add Schedule Trigger" button. Select the start date and the end date for the chosen event.

 

Next, choose how often the task will be executed; tasks may be executed after a set period of time has passed, and users may choose to execute the task after a certain number of seconds, minutes or hours have passed.

 

Users may also choose to have the task execute only one time, and the task will be executed once the start date has been reached.

 

Users may also choose to have the task executed a specified number of times regardless of the end date.
